Welcome!

We have gathered data from the most visited cities in Europe to help you deciding which is the best place for your next trip in Europe.

The data has been collected from https://www.priceoftravel.com and https://es.climate-data.org

Please select the month that you want to travel

As a guidance think how much would you pay for:

  • One night in a hostel (bunk bed)
  • Three "budget" meals
  • Three local beers
  • 2 rides in public transport
  • Visit to one famous attraction

*If you are not sure about the budget we recommend you to enter "91" (that is the maximum budget in our records) so you can see all the cities and then decide.
